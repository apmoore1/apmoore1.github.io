Abstract

An introudction to Sentiment Analysis describing the coarse to fine grained annotations, how the different annotations are different, and finishing with a lab session. The lab session covers online sentiment tools and building a sentiment lexicon using seed words and word embeddings based on Hamilton et al’s work.
