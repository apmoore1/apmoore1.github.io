About

I am a PhD at Lancaster University within the NLP group, where I am supervised by Dr. Paul Rayson and Prof. Steven Young. My main research interests are within Natural Language Processing and more specifically within Target/Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis, where I am interested in generalisability. I am also interested more broadly in areas of machine learning such as Semi-Supervised Learning and reproducibility.

I am also a member of the UCREL research centre and the Data Science Institute at Lancaster University. If you would like to know more about my work you are welcome to read my resume.
